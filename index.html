Healthy Campus@DKIT

About Us

Healthy Campus@DKIT is an initiative to promote both physical health, mental health and general wellbeing in County Louth and the surrounding locality. A Healthy Campus adopts a holistic understanding of health, takes a whole campus approach, and aspires to create a learning environment and organisational culture that enhances the health and wellbeing of its community and enables people to achieve their full potential.

Here, we will explain the benefits of both physical exercise and good nutrition, and how they can both significantly improve your general wellbeing. You can use the menu button on the top left of this webpage to navigate through each section.



Our Aim

We hope to not only inspire the people of County Louth, but also everyone who reads this to make an effort to allocate some time in improving your physical health and nutritional intake. Even if it's as little as a 5 minute walk everyday or having an apple or two everyday!

With only four in ten people having a normal weight and six out of ten people being overweight or obese in Ireland, it cannot be emphasised more that it is important that we take care of ourselves not only for us, but for future generations too!
