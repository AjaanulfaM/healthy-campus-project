Introduction to 'meats and dairy'

1.) Meat and poultry products

Meat and poultry are great sources of protein, while also providing lots of other nutrients such as iodine, iron, zinc, vitamins (especially B12) and essential fatty acids.

Some examples of meat and poultry products includes:

  • Beef
  • Chicken
  • Eggs
  • Pork
  • Turkey
  • Lamb

Benefits of including meat and poultry to your diet.

Because of the high amount of protein found in meat and poultry products, they are very good sources of growth and development of your muscles.

The other nutrients found in meat and poultry can also help your body:

  • Iodine helps your body produce thyroid hormone
  • Iron carries oxygen around your body
  • Zinc keeps your immune system strong, your skin healthy, and helps with growth, development and reproductive health
  • Vitamin B12 supports the nervous system
  • Omega-3 fats maintains your heart and brain health
